My Experience

Experience of participating in the flood volunteer team of young Kelantan MESRA

Experience

In 2022, my friend and I joined the volunteers who were given the "goyoh gaha" (rocking) position. This volunteering was about cleaning houses that had been affected by floods.

We were divided into 2 groups, namely the Tumpat and Rantau Panjang groups. My friend and I had to go to Tumpat to clean up there. After we finished, we had to go to Rantau Panjang to help out next since there were still areas where there was still floodwater that had not receded.
